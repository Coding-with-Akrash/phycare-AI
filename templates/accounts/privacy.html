Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PsyCare AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our healthcare technology platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, use our services, or communicate with us:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Professional credentials (for healthcare providers)
  • Medical information and health records (for patients)
  • Insurance information and billing details
  • Communication preferences
Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we may automatically collect certain information:

  • Device information and operating system
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage patterns and feature preferences
  • Log files and error reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our healthcare services
  • Facilitating communication between patients and healthcare providers
  • Processing appointments and medical records
  • Ensuring compliance with healthcare regulations (HIPAA)
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Sending important updates and notifications

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • Healthcare Providers: With your authorized healthcare providers for treatment purposes
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist in our operations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication options
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

6. HIPAA Compliance

As a healthcare technology provider, we are fully committed to HIPAA compliance. Our platform includes:

  • Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Audit logs and monitoring
  • Data backup and recovery procedures
  • Employee training on HIPAA requirements

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal and regulatory requirements. Medical records are retained in accordance with applicable healthcare record retention laws.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
