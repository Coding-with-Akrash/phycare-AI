About PsyCare AI

Revolutionizing healthcare through secure, innovative authentication solutions. Built by healthcare professionals, for healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

To provide secure, user-friendly authentication solutions that empower healthcare professionals and patients to focus on what matters most - improving health outcomes through better connectivity and streamlined workflows.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare authentication platform globally, setting new standards for security, interoperability, and user experience in medical technology while maintaining the highest levels of privacy and compliance.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, PsyCare AI emerged from a critical need in the healthcare industry. Our founders, with decades of experience in both technology and medicine, identified a significant gap in secure, user-friendly authentication systems for healthcare providers.

What started as a simple solution to reduce password-related security breaches in hospitals has evolved into a comprehensive platform that transforms how healthcare professionals and patients interact digitally.

Today, we're proud to serve over 10,000 healthcare professionals and 50,000 patients across 25+ medical specialties, maintaining a 99.9% uptime and zero data breaches.
